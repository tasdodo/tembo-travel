Suppliers

For East African operators and lodges

Three steps to getting direct European B2B inquiries through Tembo.

1

Apply to join

Submit your operator application with company details, licensing, fleet information, and team credentials. Our regional team reviews every application personally.

2

Get verified

Pass the 14-point verification framework covering legal registration, insurance, fleet condition, guide qualifications, safety protocols, and more. Verified operators earn the Tembo badge.

3

Receive inquiries

Your verified profile goes live in the directory. European tour operators find you through search and AI matching. Receive structured, priced quote requests directly through the platform.

No commission on bookings. Flat monthly membership. Apply as a supplier
