About Tembo Travel

Built to bridge
two worlds

Connecting Europe's professional tour operators with East Africa's finest verified safari operators — across 5 countries, with standards you can rely on.

Our origin

The gap was obvious.
The solution wasn't.

For years, European tour operators sourcing East African safaris faced the same invisible wall: a supply chain fragmented across hundreds of operators, dozens of markets, and a communication culture built on WhatsApp threads and annual trade show handshakes.

The operators doing exceptional work — running immaculate fleets, employing certified guides, upholding rigorous safety standards — were often invisible to European buyers who simply didn't have the regional contacts or the bandwidth to verify them independently.

Tembo was built by travel industry professionals who had seen this friction firsthand — on both sides. We knew that the problem wasn't a lack of quality on either end. It was infrastructure. A trusted, neutral layer that could standardise access, verify quality, and let both sides focus on what they do best.

The platform you're reading about now is that layer.

🐘
Why "Tembo"?

Tembo is the Swahili word for elephant — the symbol of strength, long memory, and deep trust across East Africa. It felt right for a platform built on exactly those principles: robust infrastructure, institutional memory for verified operators, and trust earned through rigorous standards rather than marketing.

Our values

Three principles.
No exceptions.

Every feature, every policy, every decision traces back to these.

🛡️

Trust through rigour

Every operator on our platform passes a 14-point verification framework — conducted by our regional team, not self-certified by the operator. Legal registration, insurance, fleet condition, guide qualifications, safety protocols, financial references, environmental policy, community engagement, and more. Trust isn't a badge. It's a documented process.

⚡

Speed without sacrifice

AI-powered matching replaces weeks of emails with 60-second itinerary builds. Not because we value speed for its own sake — but because time spent chasing supplier responses is time not spent on client relationships. The infrastructure should be invisible. The relationships should be everything.

⚖️

Fairness by design

Flat monthly fees. No commission. Operators keep 100% of their margins on every booking, every time. We built the model this way deliberately — because commission structures create incentives to push volume over quality. A platform that earns when you earn badly misaligns with the mission of raising standards.

The verification standard

14 checkpoints.
Zero shortcuts.

In East African B2B travel, the gap between perception and reality is where reputations are built and destroyed. A safari operator can present professionally online while carrying inadequate insurance, employing uncertified guides, or operating vehicles that shouldn't be on a game drive.

The consequences for European tour operators — and their clients — are severe: cancelled trips, safety incidents, and professional liability. The Tembo verification framework was built to close that gap before a buyer ever sends a quote request.

Every supplier on the platform is manually reviewed by our regional team across all 14 criteria. This is not a self-certification process. Operators cannot pay their way through verification. Status is reviewed and renewed annually.
