Section 08

Verification Programme

8.1 The 14-point framework

Before any Supplier profile becomes visible within the Platform directory, the Supplier must successfully complete Tembo Travel's 14-point verification assessment, conducted by Tembo Travel's regional review team. The 14 checkpoints assessed are:

  1. Business registration and legal standing
  2. Tourism board or government licence (jurisdiction-specific)
  3. Public liability and professional indemnity insurance coverage
  4. Bank reference letter demonstrating financial stability
  5. Vehicle fleet inspection report and roadworthiness certification
  6. Guide certification and professional qualification records
  7. Safety standards audit (including emergency protocols)
  8. Client reference check (minimum three verifiable professional references)
  9. Operational capacity assessment
  10. Communication standards audit (responsiveness and professionalism)
  11. Pricing transparency and rate sheet review
  12. Environmental and sustainability policy
  13. Community benefit and local employment practices
  14. Commitment to the annual review cycle

8.2 Nature of verification

Verification is a point-in-time assessment conducted at the date of review. It is not a continuous audit, a warranty of ongoing compliance, or a guarantee of the quality of any individual service delivered by a Supplier. Tembo Travel makes reasonable efforts to maintain the accuracy of verification status indicators but cannot guarantee that all displayed information reflects current real-world conditions.

8.3 Annual renewal

Supplier verification status is subject to annual renewal. Suppliers must submit updated documentation and complete a re-assessment annually to maintain their verified status.
